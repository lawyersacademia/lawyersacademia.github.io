About Us

Who We Are

We are Umer Raja and Ali Shaheen both law students who came together with one simple mission: to make quality legal education accessible to every student, completely free of cost.

We gather notes from different students around Pakistan and beyond, organize them, and publish them here so no student has to struggle alone. Our motto is simple — knowledge should be free.

📝 Have Quality Notes?

If you have well-prepared notes, share them with us on WhatsApp, we will review and add them to the website so thousands of students can benefit.
